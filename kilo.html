Kilo is a drop dead simple calorie tracking application that you can access from your iPhone or iPod Touch.

The first step to getting your weight under control is to keep a food diary to track what you are eating. If it's not easy to keep your diary updated, you are not going to do it.

All you have to do (in fact, all you can do) is enter the food you eat and the exercises that you do. Kilo will keep track of your total for the day and alert you if you go over the daily calorie budget you specify on your settings page. Stay under your target every day and you'll lose weight. It's really as simple as that.
